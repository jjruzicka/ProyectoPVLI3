Oh, no! Eye fell down a deep, deep hole!

Help him get back up and escape but, keep in mind, there's

more to this place than meets the eye.

Oh, and remember, Eye does not want to hurt anyone,

so keep away from the monsters!

Use this controls to move Eye:

- Left and right arrows to walk.

- Space to jump and wall-jump.

- Escape to pause whenever you want.
